# Data Retention Policy

## Purpose
This document outlines the data retention and deletion policies for student records and user data within the Math Scholar Tracker application.

## Data Retention Policy

### Student Records
- **Retention Period**: Student records will be retained for a period of 5 years after the last interaction with the application.
- **Deletion Process**: After the retention period, student records will be securely deleted from our servers. This includes all identifiable information, activity logs, and academic records.

### User Data
- **Retention Period**: User account data will be retained for as long as the account is active. If a user requests account deletion, their data will be deleted immediately.
- **Deletion Process**: Upon account deletion, all personal identifiable information will be removed, and the user will receive a confirmation email upon successful deletion.

## Compliance
This policy complies with applicable data protection regulations to ensure the privacy and security of all users' data.

## Review
This policy will be reviewed annually to ensure compliance with legal requirements and best practices.
