# Compliance Checklist

## GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- [ ] Data collection and usage policy is in place.
- [ ] User consent is obtained before data collection.
- [ ] Users have the right to access their data.
- [ ] Users can request data deletion.
- [ ] Privacy by design principles are applied.

## FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act)
- [ ] Consent is obtained from parents for data collection on minors.
- [ ] Educational records are protected and only shared with authorized personnel.
- [ ] Parents have the right to access and review their child's educational records.

## COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- [ ] Parental consent is obtained before collecting personal information from children under 13.
- [ ] Clear privacy policy explains data collection practices.
- [ ] Users are informed about their rights regarding their children's data.

## Data Protection Requirements
- [ ] Secure data storage methods are implemented.
- [ ] Data encryption both at rest and in transit.
- [ ] Regular data protection training for staff.
- [ ] Procedure for reporting data breaches established.

---

This checklist should be reviewed and updated regularly to ensure compliance with current laws and regulations.
